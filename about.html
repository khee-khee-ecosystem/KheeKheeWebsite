Building the Future of Influencer Marketing

One platform where brands, agencies, and creators discover, collaborate, manage campaigns, and grow together.

Khee Khee is an influencer marketing platform built to simplify how collaborations happen. We bring brands, agencies, and creators together in one ecosystem, making it easier to discover the right partners, manage campaigns, communicate seamlessly, and track performance with confidence.

Instead of relying on multiple tools, spreadsheets, and endless conversations, Khee Khee provides a single workspace designed for every stage of influencer marketing—from creator discovery and campaign management to reporting and long-term partnerships.

Whether you're a growing creator looking for opportunities, a brand searching for authentic voices, or an agency managing multiple campaigns, Khee Khee helps you work faster, smarter, and with greater transparency.
